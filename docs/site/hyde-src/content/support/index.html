Support options

Commercial

Contact the author directly if you need private consulting services regarding how to best design and implement a security architecture involving sec-wall, or if you need dedicated training sessions, either on-site or delivered online, or would like to purchase a support subscription.

Community

  • sec-wall-users is an open support list anyone can join and post their questions or suggestions; note that it's a friendly place and absolutely everyone's invited to join in and share their thoughts! You need to log into you Launchpad account, join the sec-wall-users group and subscribe to the mailing list prior to posting any messages to it (hopefully, this will help combat spam),
  • Launchpad infrastructure - if you'd rather not join the mailing list, you can always drop by the bug tracker , or the Launchpad's answer tracker ,
  • IRC - #sec-wall on Freenode network is where the cool kids hang out. It's a perfect place for both quick one-off questions and for meeting other users of sec-wall,
  • sec-wall-dev is a place for core developers to join, note that the list is more concerned with an ongoing development rather that with direct support, for instance, if you're not sure where to send your patches, send them in to sec-wall-dev but if you're looking for help with configuration, please post it to sec-wall-users,
  • Visit the author's blog and start following @fourthrealm on Twitter .
